Chakulia, known as Chakuliya, is a vibrant Notified Area Council (NAC) and the esteemed 4th Municipal Corporation within the picturesque Ghatshila subdivision of East Singhbhum district in the beautiful state of Jharkhand, India. With its charming allure, it lies just 71 km away from the bustling city of Jamshedpur, serving as a unique blend of tradition and modernity, where the warmth of the community welcomes you to explore its rich heritage and promising future.

Tourist Places

Airport

Chakulia Airport is an airport in India. Currently, the airport is non operational. In 2006, it was reported that the airport has been non-operational since it served during World War II.

Parks

Chakulia boasts a collection of five parks, with one among them standing as Jharkhand's most expansive ecological park, an impressive undertaking that entailed a significant investment of 15 crore rupees for its construction.

Kanaishwar Hill

Chakulia features a captivating hilltop attraction that becomes truly enchanting during the monsoon season when a special worship ceremony is held. It's a must-visit destination for those seeking a unique and spiritual experience amid the natural beauty of the area.

Chakulia is known for!

Hindusthan Soap Factory

In 1990, the entrepreneur began producing toilet soap. South East Chemicals is a leading detergent powder manufacturer in Jharkhand.

Gousala

Chakulia Gousala: A sanctuary for thousands of cows, offering care, nourishment, and promoting cow conservation, fostering a strong bond between humans and these animals.

Airport

Chakulia boasts a non-operational airport, a relic from World War 2, echoing its historical significance. Once bustling, it now stands as a silent witness to a bygone era of aviation.

Wild Life

Chakulia forest range hosts thousands of magnificent elephants, a captivating testament to the beauty of untamed nature.
